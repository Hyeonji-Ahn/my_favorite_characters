Sugawara Koushi


Sugawara Koushi(스가와라 코우시) was previously a third-year at Karasuno High School; for the majority of the series, he was the vice-captain and substitute setter for the volleyball team. As of 2018, he is an Elementary School teacher in Miyagi.

Appearance

Sugawara is shown to be average in height with a slender build. He has slightly thick eyebrows, light grey hair and hazel-brown eyes with a birthmark mole under his left eye. He is usually seen with a gentle smile on his face.
